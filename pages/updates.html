Affordable Housing (Latest Updates)

Signature Global to invest Rs 310 crore on affordable housing project in Gurugram

The company announced the launch of its new project-Signature Global Imperial spread over 8 acres of land parcel. It is self-funded by the company and is expected to be complete in the next four years.

The project will offer 1,141 units, comprising 3 BHK apartments. The price of each unit starts from Rs 75 lakh.

Over 60,000 houses approved for construction under Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (Urban)

Secretary, MoHUA also discussed the issues pertaining to grounding and construction of houses in different States under different verticals of PMAY(U). He reviewed the pace of completion of houses in the States and directed the officials to expedite the process and meanwhile also evaluate the implementation.

New Housing Policy aims to create 20 million affordable homes by 2025

The government has announced a new housing policy that aims to create 20 million affordable homes by 2025. The policy includes incentives for developers, subsidies for first-time homebuyers, and streamlined approval processes.

"This policy is a game-changer for the affordable housing sector," said Housing Minister in a press statement released yesterday.

Real Estate Investment Trusts (REITs) show strong growth in residential sector

Real Estate Investment Trusts focusing on residential properties have shown remarkable growth in the last quarter, with returns averaging 12.3% annually. This outperforms commercial REITs for the third consecutive quarter.

Analysts attribute this growth to increasing demand for rental housing in major metropolitan areas and favorable interest rates.
